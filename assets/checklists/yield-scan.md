# Yield scan prompt checklist

A good yield prompt should include:
- chain, niche, or protocol set
- capital size or risk profile
- whether you want stablecoin, majors, or degen exposure
- requested sections such as yield source, TVL/liquidity questions, risks, and what to verify
- a rule not to rank by APY alone

Weak ask:
- "find me the best yield"

Stronger ask:
- "Scan Arbitrum stablecoin yield options for cautious capital. Return: best-looking candidates, yield source, TVL/liquidity questions, key risks, and what to verify before deploying. Do not rank by APY alone."

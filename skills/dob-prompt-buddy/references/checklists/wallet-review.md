# Wallet review prompt checklist

A good wallet review prompt should include:
- exact wallet address
- chain
- why you care about the wallet
- requested sections such as balance picture, transfer behavior, protocol exposure, suspicious signs, and next checks
- a rule not to invent labels or identity

Weak ask:
- "look at this wallet"

Stronger ask:
- "Analyze wallet 0xABC... on Base. Return: balance picture, transfer behavior, protocol exposure, suspicious signs, and next checks. Keep claims tied to observable behavior and do not invent wallet labels."
